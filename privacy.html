Privacy Policy

Thank you for reading our privacy policy. At ARES, we take your privacy seriously. We assure our users that their data is handled securely and responsibly. Please read further to learn more about the information we collect and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

ARES does not collect personal information from its website. However, the ARES Discord bot collects the following data:

  • Server ID
  • User ID
  • Channel ID
  • Message content (for specific features, such as moderation or logging)

Why do we collect this information?

We collect this information to provide customized and efficient functionality to your server. For example, features like logging, moderation, or personalized responses rely on unique identifiers such as Server ID or User ID.

2. How We Protect Your Data

We are committed to safeguarding your data. Measures we have taken include:

  • Encrypting sensitive data.
  • Implementing security protocols to prevent data breaches.
  • Regularly updating our systems to mitigate vulnerabilities.

We will never misuse or share your data with third parties.

3. Data Removal

If you wish to have your data removed from our systems, you may request a data wipe through our support server. Once your data is deleted, this process cannot be reversed.

Steps to request data removal:

  • Open a ticket in our support server.
  • Specify the data you wish to delete (e.g., server data, user data).
  • Our team will confirm and process the request.

4. Global Chat Policy

If enabled, ARES’s global chat feature collects additional information, such as:

  • Server name
  • Username
  • Message content
  • Channel webhook URL

Why do we collect this information for global chat?

This information is necessary for the functionality of global chat, ensuring proper communication between participating servers.

How can you disable global chat and remove related data?

You can disable global chat by using the appropriate command provided in the bot's documentation. This will delete all associated data for your server.

5. Auto-Moderation

ARES’s auto-moderation feature monitors messages in your server for inappropriate or flagged content.

How can you opt out of auto-moderation?

You cannot opt out of auto-moderation if it is enabled by the server administrators. However, administrators can whitelist specific channels to exclude them from monitoring.

Message Privacy: ARES does not store or redistribute any messages monitored through auto-moderation. Flagged messages are deleted immediately as per the rules set by server administrators.

6. Further Details

For more information about ARES’s features and privacy practices, please refer to our Terms of Service. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to reach out to us at support@aresbot.com.
